Third Post On My Publii

netlifex July 8, 2021

Open the Publii app and click on the Server option in the left-sidebar to open the Server Settings screen.
Set the Domain to your Netlify URL we saw in the previous section (yourSitename.netlify.com); don't forget to choose https:// from the drop-down list.
Enter the string of characters from the API ID of your Netlify site into the Site ID field.
